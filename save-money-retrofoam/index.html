What is R-Value and Why is it Important?

According to energy.gov, an insulation’s resistance to heat flow is measured in terms of its R-value.  The higher the R-value, the better the insulation’s effectiveness.  An accredited 3rd party testing lab has calculated values of RetroFoam to range between 14.5 to 23.7 depending on the thickness of the wall.  There is no doubt that RetroFoam will maximize the R-value of your walls, which significantly reduces the amount of air leaks in your home.   The exceptional sealing qualities of RetroFoam results in immediate benefits, which in turn results in instant savings for you!

RetroFoam: The Energy Efficient Choice

According to recent studies, up to 35% of your home’s energy loss is through your existing walls.
